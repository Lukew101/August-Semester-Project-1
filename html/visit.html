Plan Your Visit

There are so many things to do and see. Therefore, before visiting the Community Science Meseum, we recommend you plan out your day.

1. Location

Kolderups vei 14, Disen, 0587 Oslo, Norway.Click on the map to find us on Google maps.

2. Admission

The entrance is FREE for all.

There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour. These tours are 70 NOK per person and include a handy printed guide of the museum.

If you would like to organise a guided tour for your group of 6 or more people, please contact us to arrange the tour.

Contact Us

3. Opening Hours

Monday: ClosedTuesday to Thursday: 10:00 - 16:00Friday: 10:00 - 19:00Saturday: 9:00 - 16:00Sunday: 9:00 - 13:00

4. Getting Here

There are several alternatives to get to the Museum. These include:

1. Take the 20 bus to Kolderups Vei and walk 150m.

2. Take the tram to Koldens vei and walk 500m.

3. Drive and use our car park. Parking cost 100kr per hour. There is no gaurantee for a spot, so street parking may also be required.

5. Accessibility

The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps. It also has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired.

6. Cafe

There is a cafe attached to the museum where you can get light lunches, soft drinks, coffee, snacks and more.

The cafe can be found to the right of the main entrance.

7. Shop

Our shop offers a range of memorabilia from the museum as well as great gifts and activity packs that allow you to continue to explore science even after you’ve left the museum.

The shop can be found to the left side of the main entrance.
